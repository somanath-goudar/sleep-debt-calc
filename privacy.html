Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the Sleep Debt Calculator collects and how it is used.

1. No Personal Data Collected

All calculations happen entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Sleep hour values you enter are never sent to a server. We have no database of user inputs.

2. Local Storage

The tool saves your entered sleep hours and dark/light mode preference to your browser's localStorage so the tool remembers your data on your next visit. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

3. No Account Required

No account, email address, or sign-in is needed to use this tool.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics (page view counts only) to understand usage patterns. No personal information is collected.

5. Cookies

This site does not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may log anonymised request metadata as part of standard server logging. See Vercel's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Medical Disclaimer

This tool provides informational estimates only. No health data is stored or shared. If you have persistent sleep issues, please consult a healthcare professional.
