How This Calculator Works

1

Set Your Target

Choose your ideal sleep hours using the slider. Most adults need 7–9 hours; the calculator flags if your target is outside CDC recommendations.

2

Enter Each Day

Type actual hours slept for each day of the week. Leave a day blank (0) if you haven't reached it yet — the tool calculates based on filled days.

3

See Your Debt

The bar chart shows daily surplus/deficit. Your cumulative debt is the sum of all days where you slept less than your target.

4

Follow Recovery Plan

See how many nights it takes to repay your debt by sleeping +30min, +1hr, or +2hrs beyond your ideal each night.
